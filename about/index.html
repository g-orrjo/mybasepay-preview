About Us

Architects of the
Flexible Workforce

We exist to redefine contingent workforce solutions by building a platform purpose-built for flexible work — empowering organizations with compliant payrolling and championing worker advocacy at a global scale.

Our Mission

To empower organizations with compliant payrolling, expand access to underrepresented suppliers, and champion worker advocacy by creating equity, stability, and opportunity at a global scale. Through technology, we enable enterprises to confidently engage flexible workforces while ensuring every worker is supported, protected, and valued.

Our Vision

To lead the global transformation of work by redefining flexible and interim labor. We are building the most trusted, inclusive, and innovative workforce platform that empowers enterprises to scale with confidence while ensuring workers everywhere receive stability, equity, and opportunity.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

These principles guide every decision we make — from how we serve our clients to how we advocate for every worker.

Advocates for the Worker

We recognize that the livelihood of thousands of workers rests in our hands. It is not just business, it's a sacred responsibility. Every decision we make considers the worker, because when workers thrive, clients succeed, communities prosper, and our industry moves forward.

Uncompromising Service

We are a people-first company with an uncompromising standard for service excellence. In a technology-driven world, we believe superior service amplified by the right tools and processes is what sets us apart and ensures we win every time.

Innovative Evolution

Technology is the catalyst that amplifies our commitment to service delivery. In an industry that is outpaced by technology and antiquated in service, we set the bar for responsive, faster, accurate, and more human experiences at scale.

Our Business Is Change

Change is constant — we anticipate it, embrace it, and set the pace for the industry. In contingent labor, success depends on expecting change, adapting quickly, and thriving in the evolution shaping the future of work now.

We Do What We Say

We honor the commitments we make by being accountable, not perfect. We foster a culture where mistakes fuel learning, ideas drive progress, and our word carries weight every time.

Disciplined Urgency

The best time is now. For us, urgency means timely acknowledgement and decisive action, while ensuring the path to resolution is thoughtful, disciplined, and complete.

Growth with Purpose

We scale with intention and focus by pursuing opportunities that are aligned with our service lines, strategy, and risk profile. We aim to drive lasting impact for workers, clients, and communities.
