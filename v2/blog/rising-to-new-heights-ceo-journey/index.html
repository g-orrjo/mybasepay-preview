Rising to New Heights: My Journey to CEO and Shaping the Future of Contingent Workforce Innovation

AA

Angela Alberty

CEO, myBasePay

This role represents a profound responsibility — a calling for industry transformation affecting millions of workers globally.

A New Chapter

Becoming CEO of myBasePay isn't just a career milestone — it represents a profound responsibility and a calling for industry transformation affecting millions of workers globally. This journey has been shaped by every challenge, every pivot, and every moment of doubt that ultimately became fuel for growth.

Worker Advocacy at the Core

My vision for myBasePay centers on reimagining how enterprises engage with staffing partners while fundamentally enhancing the worker experience. This means investing in Vendor of Record capabilities, data enrichment for accurate payments, foreign currency transaction support, and transparent pricing models that respect every stakeholder.

We're also committed to offering flexible schedules, mental health resources, and career advancement pathways. Because when workers thrive, organizations thrive.

Authentic Leadership

“Organizations flourish through authenticity. Lived experience and understanding of workforce challenges create competitive advantages that no technology alone can replicate.”

My background navigating career transitions and motherhood has profoundly informed my approach to leadership. I believe in building organizations grounded in genuine human connection rather than traditional corporate structures that often leave people feeling invisible.

Diversity as a Business Imperative

At myBasePay, we've achieved 67% minority staff representation and 80% female leadership. These aren't numbers we celebrate for their own sake — they reflect organizational values and a genuine commitment to strengthening business outcomes through diverse perspectives.

The Next Generation of Work

Younger workers demand upskilling opportunities, mobility, and flexible environments where they feel valued but not micromanaged. They want customizable benefits aligned with actual needs rather than aspirational claims. Meeting these expectations isn't optional — it's the price of admission for organizations that want to attract and retain the best talent.

Building a Village

Success requires sacrifice, and it requires a reliable team — a village — that enables you to balance ambition with values. At myBasePay, we're building that village, one where every team member can bring their whole self to work and know they'll be supported in pursuing both their professional goals and their personal priorities.
