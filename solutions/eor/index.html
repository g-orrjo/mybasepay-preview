What's Included

Everything You Need to Employ Globally.

myBasePay is built by workforce compliance experts. Our EOR empowers enterprises to scale globally with confidence, removing the complexity of cross-border hiring while maintaining a consistent worker experience.

Full Employment Liability

myBasePay assumes full employment liability—including tax, benefits, and compliance—across every jurisdiction. We act as the legal employer so you're protected from day one.

Payroll & Benefits Administration

Simplified payroll and benefits administration across regions. Accurate, on-time payroll in local currencies with tax withholding, statutory contributions, and competitive benefits packages.

Worker Classification & Protection

Ensure proper worker classification to avoid fines and penalties. We provide competitive benefits and worker protections that attract and retain top global talent.

Digital Onboarding & Lifecycle Support

Digital onboarding and full lifecycle support including renewals and off-boarding. Standardized contracts and documentation reduce time-to-hire across all regions.

Global Talent Access

Enable rapid access to global talent pools without entity setup. Hire global talent without the cost of establishing entities, with access to myBasePay's curated talent networks for critical skills.

Integrated Platform
